Paper Types

Three gallery-grade options, all printed on a 12-colour Epson pigment printer for exceptional depth, accuracy, and longevity.

Lustre Pro
270 gsm · Pearl Finish
Slightly glossy with a subtle pearl texture. Vibrant tones and excellent contrast — ideal for most photography.
Best for — General photography, gifting, everyday display
★ Most Popular
Enhanced Matte
230 gsm · Flat Matte
Rich, luxurious flat matte surface. No glare, no reflections — stunning under gallery lighting or in bright rooms.
Best for — Collectors, living rooms, gallery display
Cotton Rag Archival
280 gsm · 100% Cotton · Matte
Museum-grade 100% cotton paper. The finest available — used by galleries and serious collectors worldwide.
Best for — Fine art, heirloom pieces, collectors
